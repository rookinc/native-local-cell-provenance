## Claim

The anchor node paths of the four-state local answer-cell target are generated by an ordered offset law plus a shell-dependent lift mask. Ordinary states use lift mask [1,1,0,0,0,0]; branch states use lift mask [0,0,0,1,0,1]. Adding ordered offsets to the anchor key mod 15 and then applying node=residue+15*lift reproduces the observed closed anchor paths for O0,O1,B0,B1.
